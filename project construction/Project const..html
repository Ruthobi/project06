About us

Since 2020, we have been strongly committed to providing our customers with the highest level of service in building and construction market. Building and maintaining relationships with our customers is at the heart of our business and we have a planning and management style that is consultative and systematic. Because we employ our own qualified tradesmen and rentices we are better able to maintain high standard of quality,safety and delivery.

We give our customers confidence and peace of mind during the process of planning, design and construction. The hands-on responsibilities and team approach of company directors Micheal peters, Martins Stevens,John Nwado and David Joyce underpin our organisation with a powerful and proven synergy.

Construction Services

UpConstruction provides new construction, interior remodels, exterior remodels and building maintenance in the public and private sectors. Areas of work include retail, offices, financial institutions, transportation sites, educational facilities, government properties, warehouses, and shipping facilities. Our in-house work includes but is not limited to sidewalks, concrete pads and ramps, framing, drywall, acoustical ceilings, wood trim work, doors, frames, hardware, and pallet racking systems.
